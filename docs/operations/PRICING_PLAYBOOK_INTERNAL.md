# SanMitra Internal Pricing Playbook

Effective date: 2026-05-25  
Audience: Founder, sales, onboarding, support

## 1) Objective

Use a simple 3-tier structure that:
- Converts quickly at Starter,
- Upsells to Growth for accounting-led operations,
- Protects margin at Professional for high-touch customers.

## 2) Price Cards

### GruhaMitra (PM)

- Starter: ₹1,000 + ₹25/flat
- Growth: ₹1,500 + ₹40/flat
- Professional: ₹2,500 + ₹50/flat

Rule:
- Minimum billable flats = 20.
- Invoice formula = fixed fee + (billable flats × per-flat rate).

### MandirMitra (PM)

- Starter: ₹1,500 fixed
- Growth: ₹2,500 fixed
- Professional: ₹5,000 fixed

Fair-use:
- Starter: 500 receipts, 100 seva bookings, 3 users
- Growth: 2,000 receipts, 500 seva bookings, 10 users
- Professional: 10,000 receipts, 2,500 seva bookings, 25 users

## 3) Feature Packaging Rules

### Starter
- Core operations only.
- No advanced accounting/reconciliation workflows.
- Standard support only.

### Growth
- Full workflow needed for day-to-day managed operations.
- Accounting integration, audit-ready reporting, advanced workflow controls.
- Priority support.

### Professional
- Multi-entity, approvals, integrations, and governance-heavy operations.
- Advanced controls + custom onboarding support.

## 4) Upgrade Triggers (Use in QBR / Renewal Calls)

Move Starter -> Growth when any of these occur:
1. Need accounting-led operational reconciliation.
2. Need advanced workflow controls.
3. Usage crosses fair-use for two consecutive months.

Move Growth -> Professional when any of these occur:
1. Multi-branch / multi-entity requirements.
2. API/Webhook integration requirement.
3. Compliance/governance needs stronger approval controls.

## 5) Quoting Rules

1. Always quote Monthly and Annual side-by-side.
2. Annual prepay discount range: 10% to 15%.
3. Keep setup/migration/training as separate line items.
4. Third-party pass-through costs (gateway/SMS/WhatsApp/provider APIs) must be explicit.
5. Do not promise custom features without timeline and change-order approval.

## 6) Discount Guardrails

- Default discount: 0% to 10%.
- 11% to 15% requires founder approval.
- Above 15% only with strategic justification and written approval.

## 7) Risk Controls

- Do not under-scope implementation for Professional customers.
- Do not allow unlimited-heavy usage on Starter/Growth.
- Ensure tenant/app-key isolation and accounting guardrail messaging is included in enterprise-style discussions.

## 8) Sales Hand-off Checklist

Before onboarding hand-off, confirm:
1. Final selected plan and billing cycle.
2. Billable unit count (flats) or expected monthly usage.
3. Required modules and roles.
4. Whether payment gateway and notifications are in scope now or deferred.
5. Named admin owner on customer side.
