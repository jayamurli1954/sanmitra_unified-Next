About LegalMitra

Indian legal research, drafting, templates, and compliance workflows in one focused product.

LegalMitra is built for Indian legal and professional work: source-aware answers, structured drafting, practical checklists, and review-ready workflows for advocates, CAs, company secretaries, founders, and citizens.

Who It Serves

Advocates, legal counsel, corporate advisors, chartered accountants, company secretaries, consultants, law students, founders, and individuals can use LegalMitra for research, drafting orientation, compliance tracking, and document preparation.

Research Discipline

LegalMitra is designed around source attribution, statute mapping, procedural context, citation hygiene, professional disclaimers, and practical next steps instead of generic AI answers.

Human Review First

LegalMitra assists with research and drafting, but final filing, legal advice, tax positions, compliance decisions, and client-facing documents must be reviewed by a qualified professional.
