About LegalMitra

The dedicated Indian legal AI product in the SanMitra platform.

LegalMitra focuses on legal research, drafting support, compliance workflows, professional templates, litigation readiness, and source-aware AI assistance.

Separate Legal Experience

LegalMitra remains separate from the MitraBooks ERP frontend. Its workflow is built for advocates, chartered accountants, company secretaries, consultants, founders, and citizens who need legal orientation with human review.

Research and Drafting Discipline

LegalMitra is designed around source attribution, statutory verification, professional disclaimers, and practical filing workflows instead of generic AI answers.

LegalMitra E2E First

The SanMitra staged E2E plan starts with LegalMitra so the product can be tested, stabilized, and frozen before moving to MitraBooks ERP and later modules.
