Pricing and eligibility

Know exactly what each LegalMitra plan allows.

Use this page as the working eligibility sheet for E2E testing, free-tier restrictions, upgrade prompts, and production pricing communication.

Starter For evaluation and light professional use

Useful for trying LegalMitra research, basic tools, and a small number of documents before upgrading.

  • 5 AI legal research queries per day
  • 5 template drafts per month
  • 10 compliance tracker records
  • Chat and uploaded document retention for 30 days
  • GST Rate Finder and Limitation Calculator
Growth For regular advocates, CAs, and consultants

Designed for daily research, drafting, tracker use, and all standard Legal Tools.

  • 50 AI legal research queries per day
  • 30 template drafts per month
  • 100 compliance tracker records
  • Chat and uploaded document retention for 150 days
  • All Legal Tools and response workspace actions
Professional For chambers, advisory teams, and heavy workflow users

Unlocks the high-volume workflow path, government form auto-fill, and full tracker capacity.

  • Unlimited AI legal research queries
  • 200 template drafts per month
  • Unlimited compliance tracker records
  • Chat and uploaded document retention for 300 days
  • Official PDF upload and auto-fill workflow up to 25 MB

Upgrade behavior

What should happen when a user exceeds a plan?

LegalMitra should show a clear upgrade prompt explaining the blocked feature, the user's current plan, and the minimum plan required. This applies to AI query limits, template generation, tracker limits, official PDF upload, and Growth-only response actions.

Deployment note

Upload and history retention is capped by plan.

The backend now rejects oversized uploads and stores retained chat/upload records with an expiry date. Final deployment hardening should still add file-type validation, malware scanning, and tenant-level confidentiality controls before production use.
