About MitraBooks

Cloud ERP and accounting for businesses, CAs, and bookkeepers.

MitraBooks is part of the SanMitra platform and is designed to help Indian businesses and professional accounting teams maintain disciplined books with GST-ready workflows, audit trails, and clear review controls.

What We Build

MitraBooks focuses on double-entry accounting, invoices, purchases, receipts, payments, bank reconciliation, GST/TDS preparation, reports, document queues, and multi-company practice workflows.

Who It Serves

  • Business owners and internal accountants who need clean books and practical compliance workflows.
  • CA practices and bookkeepers handling multiple client companies from one workspace.
  • Teams that need client access control, staff assignment, document tracking, and review queues.

Accounting Discipline

MitraBooks treats posted accounting data as audit-sensitive. AI, OCR, settings, and onboarding data are designed to assist preparation, not bypass human review or ledger posting controls.
