Privacy Policy

MitraBooks privacy and data handling.

This policy explains the main categories of information handled by MitraBooks. It is intended for product transparency and should be reviewed with the final commercial terms before production rollout.

Information We Handle

  • Organization, practice, company, branch, tax registration, and billing profile details.
  • Admin, staff, client user, role, permission, and contact information.
  • Invoices, purchases, vouchers, ledgers, reports, statements, document uploads, OCR output, and audit records.
  • Usage logs, support requests, subscription details, and implementation or migration notes.

How We Use Information

We use information to provide MitraBooks accounting, ERP, reporting, onboarding, support, billing, security, and compliance preparation features. Tenant financial records are handled as tenant-scoped business data.

AI, OCR, and Uploaded Documents

AI and OCR features may extract data from uploaded documents, suggest classifications, or prepare summaries. These outputs require user review before financial posting, filing, or management reliance.

Security and Retention

MitraBooks should use role-based access, tenant isolation, audit trails, and controlled retention. Payment credentials, secrets, and unrelated personal data should not be stored in MitraBooks records.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact contact@sanmitratech.in.
