MitraBooks service terms for accounting and ERP workflows.

These terms summarize the intended use of MitraBooks. Final commercial terms, subscription orders, and implementation statements of work may add more detail.

Service Scope

MitraBooks provides cloud ERP and accounting workflows for business books, GST/TDS preparation, reports, document processing, and CA/bookkeeper practice operations.

User Responsibilities

  • Maintain accurate business, tax, company, user, and accounting information.
  • Review invoices, vouchers, reports, OCR output, AI suggestions, and statutory preparation data before use.
  • Keep credentials secure and grant access only to authorized staff, clients, or advisors.

Accounting Controls

Posted financial records should be corrected through reversals or adjustment entries instead of in-place edits. MitraBooks is not a substitute for professional CA, tax, audit, or legal judgment.

Service Limits

MitraBooks is not a live GST filing portal, bank payment execution system, payroll engine, or statutory adviser unless a reviewed integration and tenant authorization explicitly enable that scope.
