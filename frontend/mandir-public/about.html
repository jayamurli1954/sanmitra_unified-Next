About MandirMitra

Temple, trust, and NGO administration, devotee records, and accounting simplified.

MandirMitra is designed for Indian temples and religious trusts: clean devotee directories, transparent Hundi collection tracking, instant bilingual donation receipts, and fully compliant double-entry accounting.

Who It Serves

Temple administrators, trust committee members, treasurers, priests, accounts managers, and devotees can use MandirMitra to book sevas, track fund allocations, and file receipts.

Financial Integrity

MandirMitra is built with accounting discipline, ensuring all collections are logged through the MitraBooks double-entry ledger. Cash collections and online transactions reconcile seamlessly.

Devotee Seva Portal

Devotees can register, schedule auspicious sevas, receive direct mobile notification updates, and instantly download their official receipts for income tax (80G) deductions.

MandirMitra Temple Management System handles devotee registers, Hundi tracking, seva bookings, and trust accounting. Part of the SanMitra Platform.
