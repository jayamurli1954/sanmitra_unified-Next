Privacy Policy

How MandirMitra handles devotee information, seva schedules, donation data, audit records, and user rights.

Effective Date: May 28, 2026 Last Updated: May 28, 2026 Version: 1.0

1. Introduction

MandirMitra ("we," "our," or "us") is dedicated to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring compliance with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act) and other applicable personal data protection regulations in India.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, process, record, store, and safeguard your personal data when you use the MandirMitra temple management and public donation portal ("Service").

By using MandirMitra, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

Under the DPDP Act, 2023, MandirMitra acts as a Data Fiduciary for the personal data processed.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Personal Data

We collect details to coordinate religious offerings and donations:

A. Devotee & Transaction Details:

  • Devotee full name and contact information (Phone number, Email)
  • Gotra, Nakshatra, and Family details (for custom Seva chanting)
  • Donation amounts and transaction references
  • Billing address (required for generating 80G tax receipts)

B. Technical Data (Automatically Collected):

  • IP address, browser parameters, and access durations
  • Navigation steps on the public payment screen

3.2 Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not store payment card PINs or net banking credentials. Payments are securely processed through integrated gateways (like Razorpay).
  • We do not collect unrelated biometric details or medical histories.

4. How We Use Your Information

4.1 Purposes of Data Processing

We process information to:

  • Schedule and perform requested temple Sevas
  • Generate stable, auditable donation receipts (including 80G documentation)
  • Maintain double-entry accounting records in the unified MitraBooks ledger
  • Resolve transaction issues or process refunds
  • Send transaction confirmation alerts and notifications

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

5.1 Third Parties We Share With

  • Payment Gateways (Razorpay): To complete donation payments securely.
  • Government Bodies: To file tax statements for trusts and submit 80G donor statements.

We do NOT sell or distribute devotee database lists for commercial marketing.

6. Data Storage and Security

Devotee domain records (like Seva names) are securely held in MongoDB. Financial transactions are registered in isolated tables on PostgreSQL. We employ TLS/HTTPS encryption for data transit and strict server access controls to ensure maximum security.

7. Your Rights Under DPDP Act, 2023

You have the right to request access to your records, ask for corrections to receipt details, request erasure of records (where legally permitted), or designate a nominee. To exercise these rights, please email `mandirmitra@sanmitratech.in`.

8. Grievance Redressal

If you have any questions or wish to file a complaint regarding privacy, you can reach our Grievance Officer at `mandirmitra@sanmitratech.in`. We resolve all complaints within 30 days.
