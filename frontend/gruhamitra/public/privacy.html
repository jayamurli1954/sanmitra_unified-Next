GruhaMitra Privacy

Privacy Policy

How GruhaMitra collects and handles personal data, resident directories, maintenance invoices, and user rights.

Effective Date: May 28, 2026 Last Updated: May 28, 2026 Version: 1.0

1. Introduction

GruhaMitra ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring compliance with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act) and other applicable personal data protection regulations in India.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, process, record, store, and safeguard your personal data when you use the GruhaMitra housing society management platform ("Service").

By using GruhaMitra, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Personal Data

We collect details to coordinate housing society administration and billing:

A. Resident & Member Details:

  • Resident full name, phone number, email address
  • Flat/unit number, block, occupant role (Owner / Tenant / Committee Member)
  • Maintenance billing history, paid amounts, payment reference IDs
  • Complaint logs, repair descriptions, and technician assignments

B. Technical Data (Automatically Collected):

  • IP address, browser parameters, access timestamps
  • Usage logs of PWA features

3.2 Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not store payment card PINs or net banking credentials. Payments are securely processed through integrated gateways (like Razorpay).
  • We do not collect sensitive medical histories, biometric details, or personal beliefs.

4. How We Use Your Information

4.1 Purposes of Data Processing

We process information to:

  • Maintain isolated tenant databases for housing societies
  • Automate monthly or quarterly maintenance invoicing
  • Post collections atomically to the MitraBooks double-entry ledger
  • Resolve facility complaints and log repair tickets
  • Broadcast important society announcements and notices

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

5.1 Third Parties We Share With

  • Payment Gateways (Razorpay): To complete maintenance fee transactions.
  • SMS/WhatsApp Providers: To send invoice alerts and notice broadcasts.

We do NOT sell or distribute resident databases to commercial marketers.

6. Data Storage and Security

Resident details and complaint profiles are securely held in MongoDB. Financial transactions and ledger entries are registered in isolated tables on PostgreSQL. We employ TLS/HTTPS encryption for data transit and strict server access controls to ensure maximum security.

7. Your Rights Under DPDP Act, 2023

You have the right to request access to your records, ask for corrections to bill details, request erasure of records (where legally permitted), or designate a nominee. To exercise these rights, please email `gruhamitra@sanmitratech.in`.

8. Grievance Redressal

If you have any questions or wish to file a complaint regarding privacy, you can reach our Grievance Officer at `gruhamitra@sanmitratech.in`. We resolve all complaints within 30 days.
