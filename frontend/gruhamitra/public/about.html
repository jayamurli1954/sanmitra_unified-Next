About GruhaMitra

Housing society management, billing, and accounting simplified.

GruhaMitra is built to modernize and automate operations for apartments, cooperative housing societies, and resident welfare associations (RWAs). We bridge the gap between administrative chores and proper financial accounting.

Who It Serves

Society committee members, treasurers, residents, flat owners, tenants, and estate managers can use GruhaMitra to manage flats, record maintenance entries, pay dues, and track tickets.

MitraBooks Accounting

Unlike generic tools, GruhaMitra integrates directly with the MitraBooks accounting engine. Maintenance collections, vendor payments, and expense vouchers post atomically to a proper general ledger.

Resident & Member Comfort

Residents enjoy simple dashboards to raise repair tickets, receive society announcements, view digital receipts, and securely pay monthly maintenance using integrated gateway channels.
